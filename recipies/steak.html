Steak

Ingredients

meat

  • fillet steak
  • salt and pepper

souce:

  • half onion
  • 300 ml single cream
  • 1 tablespoon gluten free flour
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Pat the steaks dry with kitchen paper, and season with salt and pepper. Take a heavy-based frying pan that will comfortably fit the steaks, add the oil and heat over a high flame. Carefully lay the steaks in the pan, cooking 2 minutes one side then 2 minutes second side then 1 minute the first side then place all of then into a bowl.

  2. Add the onion into the same pan. Cook for 8-10 mins until the onion is soft. Stir in the flour

  3. Add the mustard, single cream, salt and pepper and stir it.

  4. When the souce is become thickish pour it over the steaks.
