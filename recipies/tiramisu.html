Tiramisu

Ingredients

sponge cake:

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoon icing sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanillin sugar
  • 4 tablespoon gluten free flour
  • 0,5 teaspoon baking soda
  • 10-15 ml espresso coffee

cream:

  • 3 eggs
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 6 tablespoon icing sugar
  • 3-4 tablespoon cocoa powder for the top

Method

  1. We create the sponge cake first. Separate the egg white from the egg yolk. Use electric hand beaters to whisk the egg whites until they become stiff. Next, add the sugar and vanillin sugar to the egg yolk and whisk until it becomes pale and fluffy. Mix the whisked egg white and yolk with the gluten free flour and baking soda and whisk it well. Now it is ready to bake. Put it in the oven on 200Â°C for 25-30 minutes.

  2. While the sponge cake is in the oven you have time to create the fantastic Italian cream. Separate the egg white from the yolk. Use electric hand beaters to whisk the egg whites until they become stiff. Next, add sugar to the egg yolk and whisk it, until it becomes pale and fluffy. Finally mix the egg white and yolk with the mascarpone cheese.

  3. Cut the sponge cake into there. Make layers like, cake-coffee-cream-cake--coffee-cream-cake--coffee-cream. Put cocoa powder on the top.

  4. Keep the whole cake in the fridge for a couple of hours or a day before you serve it.
