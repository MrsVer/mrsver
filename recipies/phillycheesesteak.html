Philly cheesesteak

Ingredients

  • 200g fillet steak
  • 1 tbsn oil
  • one half onion
  • salt and pepper
  • gluten free bun
  • gluten free sweet chilly relish
  • melting cheese

Method

  1. Slice the onion and the steak as thinly as possible.

  2. Heat the oil in a pan and add first the onion. Next add the meat along with a good pinch of salt .

  3. Fry until the onions are golden and sticky. Spread the sweet chilli relish on the bun and then add the cheese, the beef with onion and a bit more cheese on top.

  4. Heat it up in the microwave for 30 seconds
