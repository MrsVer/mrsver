Plain rice with peas

Ingredients

  • 1 mug rice
  • 2 mug water
  • 1 tsp oil
  • parsley
  • 30g peas
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pot and then add rice.

  2. When the rice become whiter add the water, peas, parsley and the salt on it.

  3. Boil it until the rice soak the water then swich the hob off.

  4. Wait 5-10 minutes then serve.
